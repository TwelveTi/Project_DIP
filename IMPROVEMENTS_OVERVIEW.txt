💡 HOW TO USE (Recommended Workflow)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: Load Image (2 seconds)
────────────────
📁 FILE MANAGEMENT → [📂 Open Image]

STEP 2: Try Quick Enhancement (5-10 seconds)
────────────────────────────────────────────
✨ ENHANCEMENT → Click one of:
  • Auto Contrast (good starting point)
  • Smart Contrast (more powerful)
  • Enhance Blurry (if image is soft)
  • Reduce Glare (if too bright)
  • Anti-Backlight (if backlit)

STEP 3: Fine-Tune on Sliders (10-30 seconds)
──────────────────────────────────────────
🎚️ ADJUSTMENTS → Drag sliders:
  • Brightness (if too dark/bright)
  • Contrast (if image looks flat)
  • Gamma (if overall tone is off)

STEP 4: Apply Filters (Optional, 5-10 seconds)
────────────────────────────────────────────
🔧 FILTERS → Click if needed:
  • Gaussian Blur (smooth/soften)
  • Median (remove noise)
  • Sharpen (make crispy)
  • Canny Edge (detect edges)

STEP 5: Save Result (2 seconds)
───────────────────────────────
1. Click "✓ Apply Changes" (green button)
2. 📁 FILE MANAGEMENT → [💾 Save Result]
3. Choose location and format

TOTAL TIME: ~1 minute for complete workflow
